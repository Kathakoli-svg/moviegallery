Inception

Year: 2010
Genre: Sci-Fi/Action
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Inception is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller that explores the concept of shared dreaming and the power of the subconscious. Directed by Christopher Nolan, the film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's dreams. When he is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased, he must perform an impossible task: planting an idea in someone's mind through a process called "inception." With its complex narrative structure, stunning visual effects, and thought-provoking themes about reality and perception, Inception is a cinematic masterpiece that challenges viewers to question the nature of their own consciousness.
