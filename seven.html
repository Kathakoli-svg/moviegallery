Seven

Year: 1995
Genre: Crime/Horror
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Seven is a chilling and masterfully crafted psychological thriller that explores the depths of human depravity and moral corruption. The film's dark atmosphere, compelling performances by Brad Pitt and Morgan Freeman, and its shocking conclusion make it a haunting cinematic experience that lingers long after the credits roll. It's a powerful examination of sin, justice, and the consequences of unchecked ambition.
