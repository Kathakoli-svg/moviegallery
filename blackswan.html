Black Swan

Year: 2010
Genre: Horror/Drama
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Black Swan is a psychological thriller that delves into the dark and twisted world of ballet. The film follows Nina, a dedicated ballerina who is chosen to play the lead role in "Swan Lake." As she struggles to embody both the innocent White Swan and the seductive Black Swan, her mental state deteriorates, blurring the lines between reality and hallucination. The movie's intense visual style, combined with its exploration of obsession and self-destruction, makes it a haunting and unforgettable cinematic experience.
