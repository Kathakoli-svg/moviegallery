Pulp Fiction

Year: 1994
Genre: Crime/Thriller
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Pulp Fiction is a masterpiece of non-linear storytelling that redefined cinema with its bold narrative structure and unforgettable characters. It's a film that blends crime, humor, and philosophical depth in a way that's both entertaining and thought-provoking. The iconic dialogue, memorable scenes like the dance at Jack Rabbit Slim's, and the way it weaves together seemingly unrelated storylines make it a timeless classic that continues to influence filmmakers and audiences alike.
