Requiem for a Dream

Year: 2000
Genre: Horror/Drama
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Requiem for a Dream means a funeral mass for dead dreams, signifying the tragic demise of the characters' aspirations due to addiction, self-deception, and destructive dependencies that consume their hopes, love, and ambitions, leaving them in despair and isolation instead of achieving their goals.
