Baishe Srabon

Year: 2011
Genre: Thriller/Mystery
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Baishe Srabon is a gripping thriller that explores the dark underbelly of Bengali society. The film follows a detective investigating a series of mysterious disappearances in a small town. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of corruption, deceit, and hidden secrets that challenge his understanding of justice and morality. The movie's atmospheric cinematography and compelling performances make it a standout in the thriller genre.
