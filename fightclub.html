Fight Club

Year: 1999
Genre: Action/Crime
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Fight Club is a powerful and thought-provoking film that explores themes of masculinity, consumerism, and identity in modern society. It follows the story of an unnamed narrator who becomes involved in an underground fight club, which evolves into something much more sinister. The film is known for its intense performances, particularly by Edward Norton and Brad Pitt, and its unique narrative structure that challenges viewers to question reality and societal norms.
