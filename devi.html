Devi

Year: 1960
Genre: Drama
Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Satyajit Ray’s Devi (1960) is a critically acclaimed, unsettling masterpiece exploring the dangerous intersection of blind faith, superstition, and patriarchy in 19th-century Bengal. The film follows a young wife, Dayamoyee (Sharmila Tagore), who is declared an incarnation of the Goddess Kali by her father-in-law, leading to tragic psychological and social consequences.
